Nokia Photonic Service Switch 1830 PSS

The 1830 Photonic Service Switch (PSS) is a family of DWDM multiservice, multilayer P-OTN transport platforms that provide efficient transport at any scale, from compact access to the converged Optical Transport Network (OTN)/DWDM core. With 1830 PSS, you can continuously scale your optical networks to meet unpredictable traffic demands.
